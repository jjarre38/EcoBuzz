About the Founders

Meet the passionate individuals behind EcoBuzz

Nicholas Żaczek

Co-Founder & Co-CEO

Nicholas Żaczek is a student at Georgia State University, where he has learned many skills, that he is directing into a passion project to support the environment. As a young boy, he always aspired to be a zoologist, fueled by his deep passion for animals and the environment. Although his career interest has changed, his passion for the environment has not. Through EcoBuzz, he intends to create a stronger community that is built on environmental protection. Alongside the EcoBuzz team, he will produce agricultural products, strong initiatives to promote environmental sustainability, and move to create a better community that supports the environment around us.

Preston Anweiler

Co-Founder, Co-CEO & Director of Apiculture

Preston Anweiler is a working man that has a devout passion for protecting the environment. Since a young age, Preston has had a deep love and connection for the environment. Recently, he has had growing concerns regarding the state of the environment, more specifically, the prescence of bees. This has led him to co-found EcoBuzz and promote sustainability while also being able to interact with one of his favorite creatures, bees.

"I really love bees"- Preston Anweiler
